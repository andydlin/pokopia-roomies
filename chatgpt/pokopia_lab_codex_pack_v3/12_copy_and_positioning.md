# Copy + Positioning

## One-line description
Pokopia Lab is a planning toolkit for Pokémon Pokopia that helps players find the best items, discover compatible Pokémon, and compare team setups without manual cross-referencing.

## Homepage hero
### Headline
Plan better Pokopia setups.

### Subheadline
Find the best items for your group, explore compatible Pokémon, and compare saved teams in one place.

## Feature blurbs
### Item Optimizer
See which categories and items benefit the most Pokémon in your selected group.

### Reverse Lookup
Start from an item, habitat, favorite, or specialty and instantly find matching Pokémon.

### Compatibility Explorer
Open any Pokémon and see who pairs best with it, plus why.

### Saved Teams
Save your favorite setups and compare them side by side.
